Addendum

The development of "Revenge of Lights Out" was a captivating journey that blended creativity and problem-solving skills. Our goal was to create an immersive and entertaining experience for players while incorporating unique design elements.

Creative Choices:

  • Grid Selection: The 5x5 grid was carefully chosen to strike a balance between complexity and accessibility, ensuring a challenging yet enjoyable gameplay experience.
  • Visual Appeal: The minimalist user interface was designed to prioritize gameplay while incorporating modern design principles, creating a sleek and visually appealing environment.
  • Rewarding Feedback: The modal window announcing game completion was implemented to provide players with a satisfying sense of accomplishment and a clear indication of their success.

Challenges and Solutions:

  • Randomization: Implementing an effective randomization algorithm for the board setup was a significant challenge. This was overcome by developing a sophisticated shuffling function that ensures a unique and solvable game state for each session.
  • Solvability: Ensuring that the game remained solvable after randomization was a complex task. Through rigorous testing and tweaking of the shuffling algorithm, we were able to guarantee solvability while maintaining the desired level of challenge.

Insights Gained:

  • User-Centric Approach: Throughout the development process, we realized the paramount importance of keeping the user experience at the forefront. Simple, intuitive gameplay and a well-designed interface significantly enhance player engagement and overall satisfaction.
  • Iterative Testing: Rigorous and iterative testing proved invaluable in identifying and resolving gameplay and usability issues. This experience reinforced the significance of thorough testing in game development, ensuring a polished and enjoyable final product.
